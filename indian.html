Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients: 1.5 pounds chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces, 1 onion, finely chopped, 3 cloves garlic, minced, 2 tbsp grated ginger, 1 can diced tomatoes, 1 cup coconut milk, 1 tbsp paprika, 1 tbsp cumin, 1 tbsp coriander, 1 tbsp turmeric, 1 tbsp garam masala, Salt and pepper, 2 tbsp vegetable oil, Fresh cilantro, Plain yogurt, and Juice of 1 lime.

Instructions: Heat oil in a large pan or skillet over medium-high heat. Add onion, garlic, and ginger, and sauté for 5-7 minutes until onion becomes translucent. Add chicken, paprika, cumin, coriander, turmeric, and garam masala, and stir until chicken is fully coated. Cook for 5 minutes or until chicken is browned. Add diced tomatoes, coconut milk, and salt and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes or until the sauce has thickened. Garnish with fresh cilantro, yogurt, and lime juice before serving. Serve with rice, naan, or roti.
